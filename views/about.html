ABOUT:

Costello is a psych/blues trio based out of Norman, OK. Their debut album, "Manifest", was recorded by Chad Copelin and Sam Welchel out of Blackwatch and Bell Lab Studios. Dan Millice of Engine Room Audio in NYC, New York mastered the album.


Clare Costello - vocals and guitar
Felix Rodriguez - bass
Jason Scott - drums
