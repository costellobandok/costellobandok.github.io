ABOUT:

Costello is a psych/blues trio based out of Norman, OK. Their debut album, "Manifest", was recorded at Bell Labs, produced at Blackwatch by Chad Copelin (with the help of Sam Welchel), and mastered at Engine Room Audio by Dan Millice.


Clare Costello - vocals and guitar
Felix Rodriguez - bass
Jason Scott - drums
