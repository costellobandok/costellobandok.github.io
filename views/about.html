ABOUT:

Costello is a psych/blues/garage rock trio based out of Norman, OK. Their debut album, "Manifest", was recorded out of Blackwatch and Bell Labs Studios by Chad Copelin and Sam Welchel. Chad Copelin produced the album. Dan Millice of Engine Room Audio in NYC, New York mastered the album.


Clare Costello - vocals and guitar
Felix Rodriguez - bass
Jason Scott - drums
